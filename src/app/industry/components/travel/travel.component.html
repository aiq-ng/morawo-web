Implementation of data analytic to improve the travel and hospitality sector

Data science makes it possible to set up personalized offers by analyzing geographical locations, search history, traffic, or even travel-related expenses.

In the past, the automotive industry was an engine of technical innovation. In terms of digitization, however, it took a while for many original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) to start innovating in this area.

The travel and hospitality sector is an underutilized sector, with a real economic model on which it is necessary to capitalize. We are already witnessing the transformation of the model and therefore the creation of differentiating and personalized offers such as agro tourism, sports tourism and so on.

Contact Morawo for the possible applications of data science in the travel and hospitability sector

• Personalized offers: to retain or attract customers, the offers must correspond to their situation. Big data makes it easier to offer them personalized services. This considering their location, search history, traffic, travel expenses, etc.

• Airport services: Focusing on the information provided by travellers as they pass through airports helps to effectively plan the services and resources available to airlines. By also carrying out a research, it is possible to know how customers get around, whether they visit duty-free shops, whether they are renting a car or not.

• A personalized welcome at the hotel: consulting clients' profiles on social networks or in their client files helps to quickly know who they are. Thus, on their arrival, we can welcome them as in a personalized way and treat them like regulars.

• Smart bracelets: these sensors can contain information about the wearer. By scanning them, they become accessible and allow employees to surprise tourists. For example, using their name to address them. In addition, these wristbands effectively replace the hotel room key and store credit card information which is more convenient for shopping.

• Direct billing of minibar costs: in some hotels, the minibars have a built-in scanner. The latter examines which products are available and which are no longer available. So, the consumption costs will be added directly to the stay card.
